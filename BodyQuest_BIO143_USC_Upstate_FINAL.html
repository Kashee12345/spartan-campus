⚔ HOW TO PLAY ⚔

🎮 CHOOSE YOUR CHARACTER

  • Freshman (100 HP) — Forgiving! Only lose 5 HP per wrong answer. Great for first timers.
  • Athlete (130 HP) — High HP with streak bonuses, but breaking a streak costs 18 HP!
  • Night Owl (70 HP) — Low HP but earns double XP. Momentum builds with correct answers.
  • Dr. Garg (150 HP) — Locked! Beat all 7 zones to unlock. Needs 2 wrong in a row to break streak.

🗺 NAVIGATING THE MAP

  • Use Arrow Keys or W A S D on keyboard
  • On phone or tablet, use the D-Pad buttons below the map
  • Walk into a glowing zone portal to enter a boss battle
  • The world has a day/night cycle with rain and lightning at night
  • Use ☰ MENU (top right) to return to the title screen. Progress auto-saves!

⚔ BOSS BATTLES

  • Each battle has 20 questions from the zone's topic pool (shuffled each time)
  • Answer using A / B / C / D keys on keyboard, or tap the answer button
  • Each correct answer deals 10 damage to the boss (boss has 100 HP)
  • Wrong answers cost HP (amount varies by character)
  • Read the study tip after each question to reinforce learning
  • Defeat the boss to unlock adjacent zones on the map
  • If the boss survives all 20 questions, you can retry with a fresh set
  • Victory screen shows your score (correct / total) and missed question recap

🔥 STREAKS & XP

  • Consecutive correct answers build a streak multiplier
  • Streaks award bonus XP and gold
  • At streak 5+, your hits show as CRIT with extra damage effects
  • XP levels up your character automatically

🪙 GOLD & CARD SHOP

  • Earn gold from correct answers in battle (bonus gold at 3+ streak)
  • Visit the Card Shop on the map and spend 75 gold for a 3-card pack
  • Cards come in 3 rarities: Common, Rare, and Legendary
  • Each card has an anatomy fact on the back. Tap to flip!
  • Collect all 30 cards to complete the set

🏆 TROPHY ROOM

  • View your full card collection in the Trophy Room on the map
  • Filter cards by rarity: All, Common, Rare, Legendary
  • Tap any card to see its full detail and fact

🎵 MUSIC & SOUND

  • Each area has its own unique music: map, battle, shop, trophy room, title screen
  • Use the ♪ ON/OFF button (top of screen) to toggle music

💾 SAVING

  • Progress is auto-saved to your browser. No account needed!
  • Return any time and pick up where you left off via the Continue screen
  • Use ⟳ RESET (bottom right) to erase your save and start fresh

DR. AKASH GARG
Educator • Lifelong Learner • Obstacle Course Racer

Dr. Garg didn't start out as someone who had it all figured out. As an undergraduate at Wake Forest University, he struggled with how to study effectively, spending hours staring at textbooks without a strategy, unsure how to make complex material stick. He wasn't the student who breezed through exams. He was the one pulling late shifts behind a bar to pay for school, then coming home to figure out organic chemistry by trial and error.

That struggle never left him. It became the reason he teaches the way he does.

Over the years, Dr. Garg earned a Doctorate of Chiropractic, a Masters in Pharmacology, a Masters in English, a Masters in Chemistry, a Masters in Business Administration, a Masters in Management and Leadership, a Masters in Experience and Learning Design, and a Masters in Higher Education. Each degree taught him something new, not just about the subject, but about how people learn differently and how the right materials can make the difference between a student who gives up and one who breaks through.

He has been teaching biology and chemistry since 2007. Along the way, he co-chaired an Academic Integrity Task Force and was nominated for the A. Wade Martin Innovator of the Year Award in 2023 for his work developing interactive Open Educational Resources, free study tools built to meet students where they are, not where a textbook assumes they should be.

Outside the classroom, Dr. Garg is a competitive obstacle course racer with over 150 Spartan Races completed, 37 Trifectas earned, and more than 2,200 kilometers raced across events including Spartan, Tough Mudder, Bonefrog, and Savage Race. He has competed at the Spartan Race World Championship and earned multiple Ultra OCR podium finishes. He approaches teaching with the same philosophy he brings to a 24 hour endurance race: show up, keep moving forward, and help the person next to you when they need it.

This game exists because Dr. Garg believes every student deserves materials that actually work, tools that adapt to how you think, not just what you are supposed to memorize. He built it because he remembers what it felt like to not know how to study, and he never wants a student to feel stuck without a path forward.

Thank you for using this material. The fact that you are here means you are putting in the work, and that is already more than enough.
